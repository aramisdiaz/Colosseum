Duel of Coders

Due to the outbreak of Coronavirus, you have been furloughed.In an attempt to get income after the extra $600 runs out. You have decided to register in a coding class in an attempt at a lucrative a career change.

To prepare you decide to go to your local library and find a book on coding basics.“The Art of Coding” catches your eye and you grab and open the book and start reading. The first line states “You who read this, should you be worthy, shall be selected as champion.” A flash of light instantly transports you to another world.

Before stands a figure wearing a black cloak, hiding his face except for two red glowing eye on a throne of node.js functions.Next to him sits a demonic looking huge Chihuahua with the name tag “Samuel Jackson.”

The figure speaks “I am The Dark Lord Schri. You have been summoned here to fight in the coding Colosseum tournament. Choose from there these pre-rendered items, defeat your opponents, and prove your might. Should you succeed, you may return to your world. Fail and be sent to the backend of github for all eternity. Now choose your weapon…”
